Taj Mahal

About the Wonder

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal and is a symbol of eternal love.

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers pleasant weather for sightseeing.

Travel Tips

  • Visit early in the morning to see the sunrise over the Taj.
  • Photography is allowed, but tripods are not permitted inside.
  • Dress modestly and carry bottled water.
