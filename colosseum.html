Colosseum

About the Wonder

The Colosseum is an iconic symbol of Imperial Rome. It was used for gladiatorial contests and public spectacles and could hold tens of thousands of spectators.

Best Time to Visit

Spring and early autumn are ideal, with mild weather and fewer tourists.

Travel Tips

  • Purchase tickets online to avoid long queues.
  • Visit early in the morning or late afternoon for better lighting and fewer crowds.
  • Wear comfortable shoes for walking.
