Christ the Redeemer

About the Wonder

Christ the Redeemer is an Art Deco statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. It stands 30 meters tall atop the Corcovado mountain and is a symbol of peace and Christianity.

Best Time to Visit

May to October offers dry weather and clearer skies for better views.

Travel Tips

  • Arrive early to avoid crowds and catch panoramic city views.
  • Use the official tram or shuttle for easy access.
  • Bring sunglasses and water, especially on sunny days.
